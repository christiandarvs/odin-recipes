Nuevo Cubano Sandwich

Description

This nuevo cubano sandwich combines black bean cilantro puree, sliced mango, ham, and cheese layered between panini grilled bread. A twist on the Cuban!

Ingredients

Puree

  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon chili powder
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 (15-ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained

Sandwich

  • Ham
  • Provolone
  • Mango Slices
  • Wheat Bread
  • Black Bean Puree

Steps

  1. Spread bean mixture evenly on bottom halves of the prepared rolls. Layer bread evenly with ham, mango, and provolone cheese; top with another slice of bread.
  2. Spread with butter (or grease the pan). Grill in a skillet or Panini Press until golden brown.
